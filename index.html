OVERARCHING GOAL (MONTHS 1–3)

“Build the foundation for consistent sales by activating personal networks, testing cold traffic, and showing proof of traction (reviews, UGC, early repeat buyers).”

STRATEGY-TO-ACTION FRAMEWORK

REFERRALS & NETWORK

Nurture existing relationships & leads.

NEW AUDIENCE (ORGANIC & ADS)

Reach new, untapped markets (ads + scalable content).

COMMUNITY & EVENTS

Engage in groups, partnerships, and in-person events.

TRUST & CREDIBILITY

Collect & showcase reviews, UGC, founder proof.

PLATFORM

Ownable channels & handles.

OPS (INVENTORY & SYSTEMS)

Stock, packaging, tracking, and dashboards.

MARKETING

Content cadence, email, promos.
